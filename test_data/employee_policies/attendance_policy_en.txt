Employee Attendance Policy

1. Working Hours
Standard working hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, with a lunch break from 12:00 PM to 1:00 PM.
Employees are expected to arrive on time and notify their supervisor if they will be late.

2. Leave Policy
2.1 Annual Leave
Employees who have worked for one year are entitled to 5 days of paid annual leave.
For each additional year of service, 1 extra day is added, up to a maximum of 15 days.
Annual leave must be applied for at least 3 days in advance.

2.2 Sick Leave
Sick leave requires a medical certificate from a hospital.
The maximum sick leave per year is 30 days.
During sick leave, employees receive 80% of their basic salary.

2.3 Personal Leave
Personal leave must be requested 3 days in advance.
The maximum personal leave per year is 10 days.
Personal leave is unpaid.

2.4 Maternity and Paternity Leave
Female employees are entitled to 98 days of maternity leave.
Male employees are entitled to 15 days of paternity leave.

3. Overtime Policy
Overtime work must be approved by the department manager in advance.
Overtime on weekdays is compensated at 1.5 times the hourly rate.
Overtime on weekends is compensated at 2 times the hourly rate.
Overtime on public holidays is compensated at 3 times the hourly rate.

4. Remote Work
Employees may apply for remote work up to 2 days per week.
Remote work requests must be submitted through the HR system.
Employees working remotely must be available during core hours (10:00 AM - 4:00 PM).

5. Attendance Records
Attendance is tracked through the company's biometric system.
Employees must clock in and out daily.
Missing clock-in records must be reported to HR within 24 hours.
